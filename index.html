Personal Details

Born on November 6, 1998 in Colombo, Sri Lanka, Nethmi Nisheka Roshel Rogers has rapidly become one of the island's most beloved talents.

An alumna of Bomiriya Central College and the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, she balances her academic background with a flourishing career in the arts.

She first gained public attention by winning the prestigious Jathika Rupavahini Awurudu Kumariya in 2019, which launched her journey into professional acting, modeling, and television presenting.
